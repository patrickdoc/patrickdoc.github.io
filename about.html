About

I am Patrick Dougherty, and I enjoy fixing things. It was once half-joked that I operate using depth-first search, which is probably more true than false. As a result, I often have a wealth of knowledge with narrow scope. I hope to distill some of that here to be useful to my future self and others.

My language of choice is Haskell, but I’ve written a fair bit of C, Python, and Bash. I care much more about ideas than actual code though, so I’ll work with almost anything. You can see what I am working on here.
